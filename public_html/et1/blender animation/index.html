Penguin guy gets icecream

By Samia, Myriam, Massimo, Stella, Jenny and Kiara

On one very sunny day, a young penguin was playing in the playground with his friends. After some time, the penguin started to get very hot. Luckily near him, there was an ice cream stand stationed there. After asking the ice cream man for an ice cream, he happily walk to the nearest bench to enjoy the sweet dessert. Sadly, he licked it too hard, which made the ice cream fall to the ground. Our penguin is now sad. But then, the ice cream man felt bad, so he gave him a free ice cream. And now, our little penguin is happy.
